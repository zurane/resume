Fumana Jewellers •

Overview

Fumana Jewellers is a South African jeweller that specializes in custom-made jewellery. The project involved creating a new brand identity, including a logo, business cards, and a website. The goal was to create a modern and elegant brand that reflects the quality and craftsmanship of Fumana's jewellery brand.
